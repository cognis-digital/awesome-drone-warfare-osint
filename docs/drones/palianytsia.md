# Palianytsia (turbojet drone-missile)

> A **missile/drone hybrid** developed by Ukraine in 18 months. Solid-rocket
> booster + turbojet sustainer + INS/GPS guidance. Subsonic but fast (Mach
> 0.7), low-altitude (15-500 m), 650 km range. The named is a Ukrainian
> shibboleth — a hearth-baked bread word used to unmask infiltrators.

## Notable deployments

* **2024-08-24** (Independence Day) — Publicly unveiled by President Zelenskyy.
* **2024-09-18** — [Toropets ammunition depot](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Toropets_depot_explosion) — 2,000 tons of ammo destroyed; military correspondent David Axe attributed to Palianytsia based on jet-engine sounds.
* **2024-09-21** — Tikhoretsk arsenal + airbase strikes.
* **2024-12-04** — Defence Minister Umerov announced **serial production**.

## Strategic importance

The Palianytsia is the first **Ukrainian-built standoff weapon** in the
Mach-0.7 / 600-km class. Because it is domestically built, it sidesteps
Western restrictions on use of donated weapons against Russian
territory. Lithuania has [committed €10 million](https://kyivindependent.com/lithuania-allocate-11-million-buy-palyanytsya-missile-drones-for-ukraine/) to its production.
