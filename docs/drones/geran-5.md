## Documented Western components

| Manufacturer | Country | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Texas Instruments** | 🇺🇸 US | 6 distinct chips identified — largest single supplier |
| **CTS Corporation** | 🇺🇸 US | Clock oscillators dated **September 2025** and February 2024 — proving post-war shipments |
| **Monolithic Power Systems** | 🇺🇸 US | Power-management ICs |
| **Infineon Technologies** | 🇩🇪 DE | At least one German chip identified |

CEPA observes that the Geran-5's bill-of-materials is consistent with the
broader trend in [Kh-101](https://www.globaldefensecorp.com/2024/01/01/western-microchips-still-find-their-way-to-russian-missile-manufacturers/),
Iskander, Kalibr, and Kinzhal missiles: ~70 % of Western-branded parts
are genuine, the rest are grey-market / over-production / counterfeit.

## Strategic significance

The Geran-5 is the empirical anchor for the argument that
[sanctions evasion routes through Hong Kong, UAE, Kazakhstan, Turkey, Armenia](https://thecfhk.org/research/europe-sanctions-evasion/)
remain wide open. Production-date forensics on CTS oscillators (Sep 2025)
demonstrate that Russia's procurement networks operate on a turnaround
time of **months, not years**.
