# MAGURA V5 (Ukrainian USV)

> The world's first naval drone to **sink a warship**. A multi-purpose
> unmanned surface vehicle (USV) operated by Ukraine's Main Directorate
> of Intelligence (HUR) — credited with destroying Russian Black Sea Fleet
> assets and forcing the fleet to redeploy from Sevastopol.

## Onboard subsystems

Per the Ukrainian Ministry of Digital Transformation, MAGURA V5 carries:
- Autopilot
- Video subsystems (multiple cameras)
- Night-vision optics
- Redundant communication modules
- Combat module
- Mesh radio with aerial repeater **or** satellite link

## Notable engagements

* **May 2023** — Damaged Ivan Khurs (Yuriy Ivanov-class).
* **2023-2024** — Sank multiple Project 22160 patrol ships.
* **2025** — Equipped with Sea Dragon SAM (modified R-73) capable of
  shooting down low-flying helicopters and drones — **first USV with
  surface-to-air capability**.

## Significance

MAGURA V5 forced the Russian Black Sea Fleet out of Sevastopol — a
strategic outcome achieved by a drone class costing **a fraction** of
its targets. The "Sea Dragon" upgrade demonstrates that unmanned
surface combatants can now host **anti-air weapons**, breaking the
helicopter-vs-USV asymmetry Russia previously exploited.
