# UJ-26 Bober ("Beaver")

> Ukrainian long-range one-way attack drone (OWA-UAV). Crowd-funded
> via the [Lachen Pyshe](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bober_(drone))
> campaign for the Main Directorate of Intelligence (HUR/GUR).
> Designed for **deep strikes on Russian territory** including Moscow.

## Design features

* **Pusher-prop, canard layout** with sleek fuselage and inverted tail.
* Capable of beyond-line-of-sight (BLOS) flight — possibly satellite-relay.
* Upgraded variants integrate thermal imaging + real-time video transmission.
* Vulnerable to Russian EW but **kinetic mass at terminal phase still inflicts damage**.
