# AN-196 Liutyi ("Fierce")

> Ukrainian one-way attack drone developed by Ukroboronprom in October
> 2022 as a direct analogue to the Russian-fielded Shahed-136. Heavily
> used in **deep strikes against Russian oil refineries**.

## Strategic role

The Liutyi is the workhorse of Ukraine's deep-strike campaign on Russia's
energy infrastructure. The 2024 [CNN report](https://mil.in.ua/en/news/cnn-liutyi-uavs-are-ai-guided/)
confirmed the Liutyi uses **AI-assisted guidance** for terminal targeting.

## Notable strikes

* **2024-11-25** — Saratov oil refinery (600 km range demonstrated)
* Sept 2024 — [Toropets ammunition depot](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Toropets_depot_explosion) (2,000 tons of ammo destroyed; likely Palianytsia + Liutyi mix)
* 2024 — Multiple strikes against [Russian refineries](https://missilematters.substack.com/p/ukraines-deep-strike-drone-campaign) deep behind the front line

## Component sourcing

Ukrainian official sources have not published a teardown. Open-source
observations and AeroVironment-comparable design suggest:

- **Engine**: domestic Ukrainian air-cooled boxer (4-cylinder)
- **GNSS**: u-blox or domestic Ukrainian alternative
- **INS**: domestic + commercial MEMS IMU
- **AI**: NVIDIA Jetson or equivalent (per CNN)
