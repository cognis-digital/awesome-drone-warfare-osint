> Center (STC) in Saint Petersburg, **150 Piskarevsky Street, building 5**

## Supply-chain finding (RUSI, 2023)

> "STC purchased some of the foreign components for the production of drones
> (approximately **300 million roubles** from January to May)"
> — public records analyzed by RUSI.

The Orlan complex demonstrates **multi-jurisdiction laundering**:
EU component → HK distributor → mainland Chinese reseller → Saint
Petersburg STC. The RUSI report names dozens of specific intermediaries.

## Sanctions status

STC LLC is sanctioned by:
- 🇺🇸 OFAC (multiple actions)
- 🇪🇺 EU CFSP
- 🇬🇧 UK OFSI
- 🇺🇦 Ukraine

Yet [satellite imagery shows](https://zmina.info/en/news-en/key-russian-drone-manufacturer-of-orlan-10-uavs-ramps-up-production-despite-sanctions-leaked-documents/) ongoing expansion of the Piskarevsky facility.
