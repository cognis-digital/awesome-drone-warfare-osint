# 🇮🇱 Israel / Gaza / Lebanon — drone use across multiple fronts

> Multi-actor theater with Israeli state forces, Iran, Hezbollah, Hamas,
> and Houthi-Yemen all employing UAVs.

## Israel — defensive and offensive

* IDF operates Hermes 900/450, Heron, and indigenous Harop loitering munitions (IAI).
* [Al Jazeera Sanad](https://www.aljazeera.com/news/2025/5/8/israel-retrofitting-dji-commercial-drones-to-bomb-and-surveil-gaza) reports Israel **retrofits DJI commercial drones** for bombing / surveillance over Gaza.

## Iran — direct strikes on Israel

* **2024-04-13/14** — [April 2024 Iranian strikes](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/April_2024_Iranian_strikes_on_Israel): **~170 drones + 30 cruise + 120 ballistic missiles** launched at Israel. ~99 % intercepted.
* **2024-10-01** — [October 2024 Iranian strikes](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/October_2024_Iranian_strikes_on_Israel): ~180-200 ballistic missiles only (drones largely absent), reflecting lessons learned.

Iranian drone families used: Shahed-136, Shahed-238 (jet), Arash-2.

## Hezbollah — Iranian-derived UAVs

* **Mersad-1/2** (modeled on Iranian Ababil-2)
* **Ababil-T** loitering munitions
* Commercial-grade ISR for IDF targeting (Lebanon border)

Sources: [Washington Institute](https://www.washingtoninstitute.org/policy-analysis/irans-game-drones), [FDD](https://www.fdd.org/analysis/op_eds/2024/04/17/hezbollahs-drones-and-missiles-may-become-increasingly-effective/).

## Hamas — commercial repurposing

* DJI Matrice 100, Mavic series modified for grenade-drop and ISR.
* Used in the 7 October 2023 attack to disable Israeli observation towers.
* See [WSJ](https://www.wsj.com/politics/national-security/chinese-americanit-doesnt-matter-israel-wants-inexpensive-drones-339a1355).

## Syria (cross-reference)
