# 🇱🇾 Libya — "The first drone-on-drone war"

> The Libyan civil war (2019–2020) was termed the "**largest drone war
> in the world**" at the time by Al Jazeera. UAE-supplied Wing Loong II
> drones for LNA (Haftar) clashed with Turkish-supplied Bayraktar TB2s
> for GNA (Tripoli).

## Sanctions / embargo issues

The UN arms embargo on Libya has been **repeatedly violated** by foreign
suppliers. Satellite imagery confirms ongoing drone deliveries to
eastern Libyan airbases in 2026.

## Why Libya matters

Libya was the **proof-of-concept** that:
1. Wing Loong vs. Bayraktar duel could happen in the same airspace.
2. Foreign-supplied UAS could decide regime survival.
3. UN embargoes are unenforceable without satellite + customs forensics.

These dynamics were rehearsed here before Ukraine and Sudan.
