# Nagorno-Karabakh (2020) — the "drone shock" reference theater

> The Second Nagorno-Karabakh War (Sep–Nov 2020) is the **inflection-point
> conflict** that put attritable strike drones on the front page of every
> defense ministry. The lessons there directly shaped procurement choices
> later applied in Ukraine and the Red Sea.

## Key tactical lessons (later replayed)

1. **Cheap drones defeat expensive air defenses** when SHORAD radars are scattered and uncoordinated.
2. **Sensor-shooter loops** of <2 minutes make armored vehicles unsurvivable in the open.
3. **Bait drones** (manned AN-2 converted) force enemy SAMs to fire and unmask radar.

These three lessons are the **direct ancestors** of:
- Ukraine's Shahed-136 saturation campaigns
- Russia's Lancet anti-artillery operations
- The Red Sea Houthi anti-shipping drone war
