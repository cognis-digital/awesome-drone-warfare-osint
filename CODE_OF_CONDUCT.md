# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

This project adopts the **Contributor Covenant v2.1** verbatim.

Full text and translations: <https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/>

## Our pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in
this community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age,
body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics,
gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic
status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity
and orientation.

## Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Demonstrating empathy and kindness toward other people
- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Accepting responsibility, and apologizing to those affected by mistakes
- Focusing on what is best not just for us as individuals, but for the
  overall community

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- The use of sexualized language or imagery, and sexual attention or advances
- Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate

## Special note for this project's domain

Discussion of the Russia–Ukraine war and adjacent conflicts is inherent
to this dataset. Contributors are expected to:

- Stay focused on **forensic evidence** rather than partisan rhetoric.
- Critique **records and methodology**, not the nationality of contributors.
- Refrain from doxxing, threats, or operational suggestions.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be
reported to the maintainers (addresses in `MAINTAINERS.md`). All complaints
will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. The maintainers
will respect the privacy and security of the reporter.

Consequences follow the four-step **Enforcement Ladder** from the
Contributor Covenant v2.1: Correction → Warning → Temporary Ban →
Permanent Ban.
